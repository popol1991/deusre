Assessing Instruction - Informed Consent

This assessment task is part of a research study conducted by Professor Jamie Callan at Carnegie Mellon University and is funded by the National Science Foundation.
The purpose of the research is to make it easier for scientists and researchers to search scientific data in tabular form.

Participant Requirements
Participation in this study is limited to individuals age 18 and older, and to people with research or scientific experience in electrophysiology or neuroscience.

Risks
The risks and discomfort associated with participation in this study are no greater than those ordinarily encountered in daily life or during other online activities.

Benefits
There may be no personal benefit from your participation in the study but the knowledge received may be of value to humanity.

Compensation & Costs
You are being paid an hourly wage based on the amount of time that you spend on this task. There will be no cost to you if you participate in this study.

Confidentiality
The data captured for the research does not include any personally identifiable information about you. Your IP address will not be captured.
